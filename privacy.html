PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy applies to people who use Beep Ltd apps and services. If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy please contact us.

Information stored and collected about you

Beep apps and services collect analytical information using Google Analytics and serves adverts using Google's advert services, such as Google AdSense and AdMob. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for the full details.

Beep does not store your information, other than data collected for analytical purposes, on our servers.

How we protect information

We also use the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) for all connections to and from our servers. This ensures that data isn't intercepted whilst in transit.
